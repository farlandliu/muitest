FAQ
Opportunities
  • What is an opportunity?

    An opportunity on Intern Avenue is a paid job with one of our employers. These can be either student or graduate internships, sandwich year courses, graduate schemes or full time graduate jobs.

  • What is an opportunity deadline?

    This indicates the last possible day that you can apply to a certain role before applications close.

  • What does "Potential for full time employment" mean?

    These opportunities are internships that initially have a limited duration but that can lead to a permanent job.

Employers
  • What is an employer?

    Employers are companies, NGOs or other institutions that are currently looking for interns or graduates to join them. Intern Avenue caters for businesses in all sectors and of various sizes who are seeking interns.

  • Why should I follow employers?

    By following an employer you increase your chances of getting hired. This is very valuable information for our algorithms to get to know you better and offer you the most relevant opportunities.

Applications
  • How do I apply to an opportunity?

    If you see an opportunity that you would like to apply to, tap the "Apply" button. After that, you will be asked to confirm your contact details, your availability and willingness to relocate if necessary. You also have the option to write a short cover letter here. Then tap "Apply" at the bottom of the page, and your application will be sent to the employer.

  • Why should I apply to an opportunity?

    Our algorithm will match you with the most relevant employers, however we believe you might have other reasons to apply to certain opportunities outside the recommended ones, so you should always feel free to apply to whatever opportunity you like. This will also help us to learn more about you and discover more relevant employers for you.

Messages
  • What are messages for?

    Messages are one of the first steps in the application process. From here employers can ask you further questions or arrange interviews, so it is very important to be responsive to increase your chances of getting hired.

  • How can I send a message to employer?

    You will only be able to directly message an employer after they have messaged you. You will receive a Push notification if you are sent a message. You can then reply directly to the message, either by opening the message or by tapping on "Messages", choosing the employer, writing the message and tapping send.

Profile
  • Why should I complete my profile?

    We have companies searching for candidates, but until your profile is complete they won’t be able to find you. Don’t miss out on the internship opportunity of a lifetime. Complete your profile now to allow us to match you with your perfect paid role. All of our candidates who have been contacted by employers have reached 80% profile completion (that includes a photo of yourself!)

  • Why should I complete Diversity questions? Are these disclosed?

    The diversity questionnaire help employers that care about a balanced workforce to know that at Intern Avenue we strive to have candidates from all communities and minorities. These answers are never disclosed. We never give out personal contact details or display your profile without your permission. We respect the privacy of our users.
